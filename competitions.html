What is Competitive Programming?

Competitive programming (CP for short) involves the application of programming knowledge and algorithms to solve very specific problems. CP is a great way to develop intuition and problem-solving skills that may prove useful down the road.

What is USACO?



USACO (United States of America Computing Olympiad) is the most prestigious and well-known programming competition for high school students. All students begin in the Bronze division and work there way up to Silver, Gold, and Platinum divisions.

There are 4 annual contests (December, January, February, US Open) where students have a chance to be promoted to the next division. The contest format is usually 3 problems in 4 hours. Each problem has a certain number of test cases that if passed, contribute to your overall score out of 1000.
